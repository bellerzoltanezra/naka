Advanced Imaging (AI) sensors:
The new SmartSENS SC3335AI, a 3MP sensor (2312H x 1304V, max30fps) with MIPI and parallel outputs, features SmartSens SmartPixel™ technology for reliable, high-quality imaging. It offers richer details and better image quality compared to 2MP sensors. Very good and sharp imaging at night, with AI Human detection and low false alarm rate.
Award winning technologies:
  • SFCPixel®
    SmartSens unique SFCPixel® technology obtains higher voltages by shortening the distance between the Source Follower (SF) and the PhotoDiode (PD), achieving higher sensitivity and better night vision imaging.
  • PixGain™
    Combining High Gain and Low Gain, PixGain Technologies achieve exceptional imaging at night and in low-light conditions while capturing fine details during daytime and in glaring light.
  • Lightbox IR®
    NIR Enhancement Technology: 2x efficiency in the infrared light range between 850 and 940 nm.
The SmartSENS Won the 'Sensor of the Year' at the 2023 World Electronics Achievement Awards.

Technical Specifications:
  • Model: Nakavision C1 - Dual lens, 3+3 Megapixel indoor/outdoor Pan/Tilt IP camera
  • System: Dual-core 32-bit DSP (XM535x), Pure Hard Compression, Watchdog
  • Sensor: 2x3MP, total 6,000,000 pixels 1/3" SmartSens SC3335AI (Advanced Imaging™) high-performance CMOS
  • Minimum illumination: Color mode 0.01 Lux @(F1.2, AGC ON), 0 Lux with IR / B&W mode 0.001 Lux @(F1.2, AGC ON), 0 Lux with IR
  • Video: H.265AI encoding to SD, dual-stream, AVI format; bitrate: 0.1M~10Mbps; framerate 1~30 fps; Streams: H264 - Main stream: 2560x1440@12fps; Sub-stream: 800x448@12fps
  • LED: 12 high-power LEDs (8 infrared + 4+4 white light), night color recording supported
  • Day/Night Switching: Supported, adjustable sensitivity IR-CUT filter
  • Noise Reduction: Hardware supported, 2D/3D
  • WDR (Wide Dynamic Range Mode): Hardware supported (optimal imaging in very bright/dark environments)
  • Sound System: G.711A codec, 2-way intercom, audio-video synchronization
  • Sound Power: Input level 2Vp-p 1kΩ, output level 30mW 16Ω
  • Wired network: 1x RJ45 Ethernet interface, 10/100M, PoE injector compatible (shielded Cat5e cable recommended)
  • Wireless network: 1x WiFi antenna, Bluetooth
  • Motion System: Gimbal, self-calibrating, 1 horizontal and 1 vertical motor
  • Hard Reset: Supported, 1-button reset (next to SD card slot, 10 sec hold)
  • Supported OS: Android, iOS, iPadOS, Windows, MacOS (in some cases with third-party software)
  • Supported Software: iCSee, XMeye and ONVIF compatible software (Win: BlueIris, Mac: SecuritySpy)
